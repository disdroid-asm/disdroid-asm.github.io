## [USB50](https://usb50-zx-spectrum.itch.io/) By Animated Alex
Animated Alex's ___[YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@animatedAL)___ is what renewed my interest in Spectrum programming.


He's recently announced that he's accepting entries for his ___[USB50](https://usb50-zx-spectrum.itch.io/)___, a compilation of 
user-submitted games written in BASIC (although small routines written in machine code are permitted).

## [USB50 Toolkit](https://usb50-zx-spectrum.itch.io/usb50)

There's also a ___[set of tools](https://usb50-zx-spectrum.itch.io/usb50)___ to assist developers.


Alex also does live streams every weekend where he creates games in BASIC - it's amazing to see
what can be done. 


Make sure to subscribe and share!
